About Me

I'm currently an industrial designer, but I've also worked in 3D animation and video editing.

Constantly trying to answer the questions "why?" and "What if?", I've been making and breaking things most of my life. I don't hesitate to question the status quo and I pride myself on keeping an open mind. For me, there is nothing better than identifying a problem and creating a solution.

I enrolled in Thinkful's FEWD course because I'm interested in digital design and I've always wanted to learn how to build my own websites!
